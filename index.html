Our Community

We are an active community of adult gamers who have united in our love for Minecraft. We also play other multiplayer games together, party games, and just hang out in voice chat!

Minecraft

We offer several Minecraft servers, a vanilla, a modded, and occasionally some adventure maps.

Voice Chat

We love hanging out in VC, whether we're playing games, watching movies, or just chit-chatting about life!

Other Game Servers

Minecraft isn't our only love, and we will occasionally offer limited-time game servers of some of our favourites like Ark and Valheim.

Watch Parties

Whether it be the latest Marvel show, a Holiday themed movie, or Minecraft Live, it's always more fun with friends by your side!

Party Games

Jackbox, browser games, Goose Goose Duck, we love our party games!

21+ Only
